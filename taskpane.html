⚠️ PayTM Safe Send
Status
✓ Active and monitoring all sent emails
PayTM Safe Send is active and will warn you before sending emails to external (non-PayTM) recipients.
Protected Domain
Trusted Domain: paytm.com
Only recipients with @paytm.com email addresses are considered internal. All other domains will trigger a warning before send.
Features
  • Warns before sending to external recipients
  • Shows list of external recipient emails
  • Requires user confirmation to proceed
  • Protects Gmail and Office 365 accounts
  • Works on Mac and Windows
  • Automatic DLP enforcement
How It Works
1. You compose an email and click Send

2. PayTM Safe Send checks all recipients

3. If external recipients found:
A warning dialog appears showing the external email addresses. You can choose to:
• Continue - Send the email anyway
• Cancel - Stop and edit recipients
